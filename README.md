# 🏨 Hotel Booking Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data to identify the factors that influence booking cancellations and provide actionable business recommendations. The analysis combines SQL for business queries, Python for exploratory data analysis (EDA), and Power BI for dashboard visualization.

The goal is not only to build a dashboard, but also to demonstrate an end-to-end data analytics workflow from business understanding to executive recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Hotel booking cancellations can negatively impact room occupancy, revenue forecasting, and operational planning. Understanding why customers cancel their reservations is essential for improving business performance.

This project aims to answer several business questions such as:

* What is the overall cancellation rate?
* Which factors contribute most to booking cancellations?
* Are there seasonal booking patterns?
* Which customer segments have the highest cancellation rates?
* What business strategies could reduce cancellations?

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Source:** Kaggle – Hotel Booking Demand
* **Records:** ~119,000 hotel bookings
* **Features:** 32 columns

The dataset contains booking information for both Resort Hotels and City Hotels, including customer characteristics, reservation details, stay duration, booking channels, and cancellation status.
